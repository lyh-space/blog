具有连续权重的感知机在二分类任务中的泛化误差

发表于 2023-07-05 更新于 2023-07-20 分类于 Note

Abstract
我们在老师学生模型和贝叶斯最优框架中分析了具有连续权重的感知机在二分类任务中的泛化误差。我们首先基于信念传播方程（BP）推导了广义消息传递方程（GAMP），通过迭代得到不同数据量密度时的泛化误差；然后基于消息传递方程推导出了状态演化方程（SE），通过迭代得到了泛化误差随数据量密度变化的理论曲线，与使用广义消息传递方程得到的结果吻合；最后我们使用复本方法进行计算分析，验证了状态演化方程的结果，并且分析了在数据量密度趋于无穷大时泛化误差的渐近行为。

1. Introduction

1.1 模型设定

在老师-学生模型中考虑一个 \(N-1\) 的二分类感知机，设输入数据为 \(\mathbf{X}\)，老师的权重 \(\mathbf{w}^{*}\) 是 ground truth，并且满足高斯先验 \(P_{\mathbf{w}^{*}} \sim \mathcal{N} \left(0, 1\right)\)，老师通过 \[ y=\mathrm{sign} \left( \frac{1}{\sqrt{n}} \mathbf{Xw}^\star \right) \] 生成标签；学生根据数据学习老师的权重 \(\mathbf{\hat{w}}\)，即 \(P(\mathbf{w}|\mathbf{y},\mathbf{X})\)。在贝叶斯最优框架中有 \[ P(\mathbf{w}|\mathbf{y} ,\mathbf{X})=\frac{P(\mathbf{y} |\mathbf{X},\mathbf{w}) P(\mathbf{w})}{P(\mathbf{y}, \mathbf{X})}=\frac{1}{\mathcal{Z}} P(\mathbf{y} |\mathbf{X},\mathbf{w}) P(\mathbf{w}) \] 其中， \[ P(\mathbf{y} |\mathbf{X},\mathbf{w}) = 1 \left[ y=\mathrm{sign} \left( \frac{1}{\sqrt{n}} \mathbf{Xw} \right) \right] \]

我们共使用 \(d\) 套数据进行训练，定义数据量密度 \(\alpha = d/n\)。

二分类任务的泛化误差（即预测标签和真实标签不匹配的概率）定义为 \[ \varepsilon_{\text{gen}} = \lim _{n \to \infty} \mathbb{E}_{\mathbf{y},\mathbf{X}} 1 [\mathbf{y} \neq \hat{y}(\hat{\mathbf{w}}(\alpha) ; \mathbf{x})] = \mathbb{E}_{\mathbf{y},\mathbf{X}} \left[ \Theta(-\mathbf{y}\mathbf{\hat{y}}) \right] = \mathbb{E}_{\mathbf{y},\mathbf{X}} \left[ \Theta(-\mathbf{z}\mathbf{\hat{z}}) \right] \]

其中 \(\mathbf{z} = \mathbf{Xw}^\star / \sqrt{n}\)，\(\mathbf{\hat{z}} = \mathbf{X\hat{w}} / \sqrt{n}\)。注意到向量 \((\mathbf{z},\mathbf{\hat{z}})\) 在对所有可能的真实权重 \(\mathbf{w}^\star\) 和输入数据 \(\mathbf{X}\) 取平均后满足高斯分布 \(\mathcal{N}(0,\sigma)\)，其中协方差矩阵 \[ \sigma=\lim _{n \rightarrow \infty} \mathbb{E}_{\mathbf{w}^*, X} \frac{1}{n}\left[\begin{array}{cc} \mathbf{w}^{* \top} \mathbf{w}^* & \mathbf{w}^{* \top} \hat{\mathbf{w}} \\ \mathbf{w}^{* \top} \hat{\mathbf{w}} & \hat{\mathbf{w}}^{\top} \hat{\mathbf{w}} \end{array}\right] \equiv\left[\begin{array}{cc} \sigma_{\mathbf{w}^*} & \sigma_{\mathbf{w}^* \hat{\mathbf{w}}} \\ \sigma_{\mathbf{w}^* \hat{\mathbf{w}}} & \sigma_{\hat{\mathbf{w}}} \end{array}\right] \] 将 \(\mathbf{z}\) 和 \(\mathbf{\hat{z}}\) 用标准高斯分布重参数化为 \[ \begin{aligned} \hat{\mathbf{z}} & =\sqrt{\sigma_{\hat{\mathbf{w}}}} \mathbf{x}_1 \\ \mathbf{z} & =\sqrt{\frac{\sigma_{\mathbf{w}^* \hat{\mathbf{w}}}}{\sigma_{\hat{\mathbf{w}}}}} \mathbf{x}_1+\sqrt{\sigma_{\mathrm{w}^*}^2-\frac{\sigma_{\mathbf{w}^* \hat{\mathbf{w}}}}{\sigma_{\hat{\mathbf{w}}}}} \mathbf{x}_2 \end{aligned} \] 其中 \(\mathbf{x}_1,\mathbf{x}_2 \sim \mathcal{N}(0,1)\)。因此泛化误差可以重写为 \[ \begin{aligned} \varepsilon_{\text{gen}} & = \mathbb{E}_{\mathbf{y},\mathbf{X}} \left[ \Theta(-\mathbf{z}\mathbf{\hat{z}}) \right] \\ & =\int D \mathbf{x}_1 \int D \mathbf{x}_2 \Theta\left(-\sqrt{\sigma_{\hat{\mathbf{w}}}} \mathbf{x}_1\left(\sqrt{\frac{\sigma_{\mathbf{w}^* \hat{\mathbf{w}}}^2}{\sigma_{\hat{\mathbf{w}}}}} \mathbf{x}_1+\sqrt{\sigma_{\mathbf{w}^*}^2-\frac{\sigma_{\mathbf{w}^* \hat{\mathbf{w}}}^2}{\sigma_{\hat{\mathbf{w}}}}} \mathbf{x}_2\right)\right) \\ & =\int D \mathbf{x}_1 \int D \mathbf{x}_2 \Theta\left(-\mathbf{x}_1\right) \Theta\left(\sqrt{\frac{\sigma_{\mathbf{w}^* \hat{\mathbf{w}}}^2}{\sigma_{\hat{\mathbf{w}}}}} \mathbf{x}_1+\sqrt{\sigma_{\mathbf{w}^*}^2-\frac{\sigma_{\mathbf{w}^* \hat{\mathbf{w}}}^2}{\sigma_{\hat{\mathbf{w}}}}} \mathbf{x}_2\right) \\ & =\frac{1}{\pi} \arccos \left(\frac{\sigma_{\mathbf{w}^* \hat{\mathbf{w}}}}{\sqrt{\rho_{\mathbf{w}^{\star}} \sigma_{\hat{\mathbf{w}}}}}\right) \end{aligned} \] 其中， \[ \rho_{\mathbf{w}^{\star}} = \sigma_{\mathbf{w}^*} \equiv \lim _{n \rightarrow \infty} \mathbb{E}_{\mathbf{w}^{\star}} \frac{1}{n}\left\|\mathbf{w}^{\star}\right\|_2^2 \] 并且根据Nishimori条件，在贝叶斯最优中有 \[ \sigma_{\mathbf{w}^{\star} \hat{\mathbf{w}}} = \lim _{n \rightarrow \infty} \mathbb{E}_{\mathbf{w}^{\star}, \mathrm{X}} \frac{1}{n} \hat{\mathbf{w}}^{\top} \mathbf{w}^{\star} = q = m = \sigma_{\hat{\mathbf{w}}} = \lim _{n \rightarrow \infty} \mathbb{E}_{\mathbf{w}^{\star}, \mathrm{X}} \frac{1}{n}\|\hat{\mathbf{w}}\|_2^2 \] 因此泛化误差最终的形式为 \[ \varepsilon_{\text{gen}} = \frac{1}{\pi} \arccos \left(\sqrt{\frac{q}{\rho_{\mathbf{w}^{\star}}}}\right) \]

2. 近似消息传递

根据式 (\(\ref{eq:贝叶斯最优框架}\)) 的因子连乘形式，容易写出BP方程： \[ \begin{aligned} & m_{i \rightarrow \mu}\left(w_i\right)=\frac{1}{z_{i \rightarrow \mu}} P_{\text{0}}\left(w_i\right) \prod_{\gamma \neq \mu} m_{\gamma \rightarrow i}\left(w_i\right) \\ & m_{\mu \rightarrow i}\left(w_i\right)=\frac{1}{z_{\mu \rightarrow i}} \int \prod_{j \neq i}\mathrm{~d} w_j P_{\text {out }}\left(y_\mu \mid \frac{1}{\sqrt{n}} \sum_{l=1}^n w_{l} X_{l\mu}\right) m_{j \rightarrow \mu}\left(w_j\right) \end{aligned} \]

为了方便推导，我们在下面的过程中将 \(\frac{1}{\sqrt{n}} \mathbf{wX}\) 中的 \(\frac{1}{\sqrt{n}}\) 吸收到 \(\mathbf{X}\) 中，最后再根据 \(\mathbf{X}\) 的order确定各方程的scaling。\ 为了导出relaxed BP方程，我们假设消息是高斯分布的，\(m_{i\to \mu}\) 的均值和方差为 \[ \begin{aligned} & \hat{w}_{i \rightarrow \mu} \equiv \int \mathrm{d} w_i ~ m_{i \rightarrow \mu}\left(w_i\right) w_i \\ & v_{i \rightarrow \mu} \equiv \int \mathrm{d} w_i ~ m_{i \rightarrow \mu}\left(x_i\right) w_i^2 -\hat{w}_{i \rightarrow \mu}^2 \end{aligned} \]

在中心极限定理下有 \[ \sum_{j\neq i}X_{\mu j}w_j \sim\mathcal{N}\left( \omega_{\mu\to i},V_{\mu\to i} \right) \]

其中均值和方差分别为 \[ \begin{aligned} & \omega_{\mu\to i} = \sum_{j\neq i}X_{\mu j} \hat{w}_{j\to\mu} \\ & V_{\mu\to i}=\sum_{j\neq i}X_{\mu j}^2 v_{j\to\mu} \end{aligned} \]

因此式 (\(\ref{eq:m_i_to_mu}\)) 可以改写为 \[ m_{\mu \rightarrow i}\left(w_i\right) \propto \int d z_\mu P_{\text {out }}\left(y_\mu | z_\mu\right) \exp\left\{-\frac{\left(z-\omega_{\mu \rightarrow i}-X_{\mu i} w_i\right)^2}{2 V_{\mu \rightarrow i}}\right\} \]

根据展开式 \[ \left(z-\omega_{\mu \rightarrow i}-X_{\mu i} w_i\right)^2=\left(z-\omega_{\mu \rightarrow i}\right)^2+X_{\mu i}^2 w_i^2-2\left(z-\omega_{\mu \rightarrow i}\right) X_{\mu i} w_i \]

考虑到 \(X_{\mu i}\sim\mathcal{O}(1/\sqrt{n})\)，做泰勒展开 \[ \begin{aligned} &\exp\left\{-\frac{\left(z-\omega_{\mu \rightarrow i}-X_{\mu i} w_i\right)^2}{2 V_{\mu \rightarrow i}}\right\}\\ &= \exp\left\{-\frac{\left(z-\omega_{\mu \rightarrow i}\right)^2+X_{\mu i}^2 w_i^2-2\left(z-\omega_{\mu \rightarrow i}\right) X_{\mu i}w_i}{2 V_{\mu \rightarrow i}}\right\}\\ &=\exp\left\{-\frac{\left(z-\omega_{\mu \rightarrow i}\right)^2}{2 V_{\mu \rightarrow i}}\right\} \exp\left\{-\frac{X_{\mu i}^2 w_i^2-2\left(z-\omega_{\mu \rightarrow i}\right) X_{\mu i} w_i}{2 V_{\mu \rightarrow i}}\right\}\\ &=\exp\left\{-\frac{\left(z-\omega_{\mu \rightarrow i}\right)^2}{2 V_{\mu \rightarrow i}}\right\} \left( 1+X_{\mu i}^2 w_i^2-2\left(z-\omega_{\mu \rightarrow i}\right) X_{\mu i} w_i+\frac{1}{2}\left(z-\omega_{\mu \rightarrow i}\right)^2 X_{\mu i}^2 w_i^2+\mathcal{O}(\frac{1}{n}) \right) \end{aligned} \]

因此 \[ \begin{aligned} m_{\mu \rightarrow i}\left(w_i\right) =& \frac{1}{z} \int d z_\mu P_{\text {out }}\left(y_\mu | z_\mu\right) \exp\left\{-\frac{\left(z-\omega_{\mu \rightarrow i}\right)^2}{2 V_{\mu \rightarrow i}}\right\} \\ & \times \left( 1+X_{\mu i}^2 w_i^2-2\left(z-\omega_{\mu \rightarrow i}\right) X_{\mu i} w_i+\frac{1}{2}\left(z-\omega_{\mu \rightarrow i}\right)^2 X_{\mu i}^2 w_i^2+\mathcal{O}(\frac{1}{d}) \right)\\ =&\frac{1}{z} \int d z_\mu P_{\text {out }}\left(y_\mu | z_\mu\right) ~\exp\left\{-\frac{\left(z-\omega_{\mu \rightarrow i}\right)^2}{2 V_{\mu \rightarrow i}}\right\}\\ &+ \frac{1}{z} \int d z_\mu P_{\text {out }}\left(y_\mu | z_\mu\right) ~X_{\mu i}^2 w_i^2 ~ \exp\left\{-\frac{\left(z-\omega_{\mu \rightarrow i}\right)^2}{2 V_{\mu \rightarrow i}}\right\} \\ &- \frac{1}{z} \int d z_\mu P_{\text {out }}\left(y_\mu | z_\mu\right) ~2\left(z-\omega_{\mu \rightarrow i}\right) X_{\mu i} w_i~ \exp\left\{-\frac{\left(z-\omega_{\mu \rightarrow i}\right)^2}{2 V_{\mu \rightarrow i}}\right\}\\ &+ \frac{1}{z} \int d z_\mu P_{\text {out }}\left(y_\mu | z_\mu\right) ~\frac{1}{2}\left(z-\omega_{\mu \rightarrow i}\right)^2 X_{\mu i}^2 w_i^2~ \exp\left\{-\frac{\left(z-\omega_{\mu \rightarrow i}\right)^2}{2 V_{\mu \rightarrow i}}\right\} \end{aligned} \]

定义输出函数 \[ f_{\text{out}}(\omega, y, V) \equiv \frac{\int \mathrm{d} z P_{\text {out }}(y | z)(z-\omega) e^{-\frac{(z-\omega)^2}{2 V}}}{V \int \mathrm{d} z P_{\text {out }}(y | z) e^{-\frac{(z-\omega)^2}{2 V}}} \]

并且根据 \[ \frac{\int \mathrm{d} z P_{\text {out }}(y | z)(z-\omega)^2 e^{-\frac{(z-\omega)^2}{2 V}}}{V^2 \int \mathrm{d} z P_{\text {out }}(y | z) e^{-\frac{(z-\omega)^2}{2 V}}}=\frac{1}{V}+\partial_\omega f_{\text{out}}(\omega, y, V)+f_{\text{out}}^2(\omega, y, V) \]

有 \[ \partial_\omega f_{\text{out}}(\omega, y, V) = \frac{\int \mathrm{d} z P_{\text {out }}(y | z)(z-\omega)^2 e^{-\frac{(z-\omega)^2}{2 V}}}{V^2 \int \mathrm{d} z P_{\text {out }}(y | z) e^{-\frac{(z-\omega)^2}{2 V}}} - \frac{1}{V} - f_{\text{out}}^2(\omega, y, V) \]

令 \[ \begin{aligned} & B_{\mu \rightarrow i}^t= X_{\mu i}~ f_{\text{out}}\left(\omega_{\mu \rightarrow i}^t, y_\mu, V_{\mu \rightarrow i}^t\right) \\ & A_{\mu \rightarrow i}^t=-X_{\mu i}^2~\partial_\omega f_{\text{out}}\left(\omega_{\mu \rightarrow i}^t, y_\mu, V_{\mu \rightarrow i}^t\right) \end{aligned} \]

式 (\(\ref{eq:m_i_to_mu2}\)) 改写为 \[ m_{\mu \rightarrow i}\left(t, x_i\right)=\sqrt{\frac{A_{\mu \rightarrow i}^t}{2 \pi N}} \exp\left\{-\frac{x_i^2}{2 N} A_{\mu \rightarrow i}^t+B_{\mu \rightarrow i}^t \frac{x_i}{\sqrt{N}}-\frac{\left(B_{\mu \rightarrow i}^t\right)^2}{2 A_{\mu \rightarrow i}^t}\right\} \]

类似的，定义 \[ \begin{aligned} \Sigma^{t+1}_{\mu \rightarrow i} & =\frac{1}{\sum_\mu A_{\mu \rightarrow i}^{t+1}} \\ R^{t+1}_{\mu \rightarrow i} & =\frac{\sum_\mu B_{\mu \rightarrow i}^{t+1}}{\sum_\mu A_{\mu \rightarrow i}^{t+1}} \end{aligned} \]

式 (\(\ref{eq:m_mu_to_i}\)) 改写为 \[ m_{i \rightarrow \mu}\left(w_i\right) \propto P_0\left(w_i\right) e^{-\frac{\left(w_i-R_{i \rightarrow \mu}\right)^2}{2 \Sigma_{i \rightarrow \mu}}} \]

定义函数 \[ f_{\text{w}} \equiv \frac{\int \mathrm{d} w~ w P_0(w) e^{-\frac{(w-R)^2}{2 \Sigma}}}{\int \mathrm{d} w~ P_0(w) e^{-\frac{(w-R)^2}{2 \Sigma}}} = \frac{\Sigma }{1+R} \]

\(\hat{w}\) 和 \(v\) 通过下式更新 \[ \begin{aligned} &\hat{w}_{\mu\to i} = f_{\text{w}}\left( \Sigma,R \right) = \frac{\Sigma }{1+R}\\ &v_{\mu\to i} = \partial_{R} f_{\text{w}}\left( \Sigma,R \right) = \frac{1}{1+R} \end{aligned} \]

至此我们推导出了 r-BP 方程，下面继续化简推导 GAMP 方程。

注意到式 (\(\ref{eq:V方差}\)) 中 \(X^2\sim \mathcal{O}(1/n)\)，说明 \(V\) 的连接比较弱，做近似 \[ V_\mu^{t+1}=\sum_i X_{\mu i}^2 v_{i \rightarrow \mu}^t \approx \sum_i X_{\mu i}^2 v_i^t \]

式 (\(\ref{eq:Sigma}\)) 可以化简为 \[ \left(\Sigma_i\right)^{t+1} =\left[-\sum_\mu X_{\mu i}^2 \partial_\omega f_{\mathrm{out}}\left(\omega_\mu^{t+1}, y_\mu, V_\mu^{t+1}\right)\right]^{-1} \]

输出函数 (\(\ref{eq:输出函数}\)) 化简为 \[ \begin{aligned} f_{\mathrm{out}}\left(\omega_{\mu \rightarrow i}^{t+1}, y_\mu, V_{\mu \rightarrow i}^{t+1}\right) & \approx f_{\mathrm{out}}\left(\omega_\mu^{t+1}, y_\mu, V_\mu^{t+1}\right)-X_{\mu i} \hat{w}_{i \rightarrow \mu}^t \partial_\omega f_{\mathrm{out}}\left(\omega_\mu^{t+1}, y_\mu, V_\mu^{t+1}\right) \\ & \approx f_{\mathrm{out}}\left(\omega_\mu^{t+1}, y_\mu, V_\mu^{t+1}\right)-X_{\mu i} \hat{w}_i^t \partial_\omega f_{\mathrm{out}}\left(\omega_\mu^{t+1}, y_\mu, V_\mu^{t+1}\right) \end{aligned} \]

因此式 (\(\ref{eq:R}\)) 可以化简为 \[ \begin{aligned} R_i^{t+1} &=\left(\Sigma_i\right)^{t+1} \times\left[\sum_\mu X_{\mu i} f_{\mathrm{out}}\left(\omega_\mu^{t+1}, y_\mu, V_\mu^{t+1}\right)-X_{\mu i}^2 \hat{w}_i^t \partial_\omega f_{\mathrm{out}}\left(\omega_\mu^{t+1}, y_\mu, V_\mu^{t+1}\right)\right] \\ & =\hat{w}_i^t+\left(\Sigma_i\right)^{t+1} \sum_\mu X_{\mu i} f_{\mathrm{out}}\left(\omega_\mu^{t+1}, y_\mu, V_\mu^{t+1}\right) \end{aligned} \]

式 (\(\ref{eq:w的更新}\)) 可以化简为 \[ \begin{aligned} \hat{w}_{i \rightarrow \mu}^t & =f_{\text{w}}\left(R_{i \rightarrow \mu}^t, \Sigma_{i \rightarrow \mu}\right) \approx f_{\text{w}}\left(R_{i \rightarrow \mu}^t, \Sigma_i\right) \\ & \approx f_{\text{w}}\left(R_i^t, \Sigma_i\right)-B_{\mu \rightarrow i}^t \partial_R f_{\text{w}} \left(R_i^t, \Sigma_i\right) \\ & \approx \hat{w}_i^t-f_{\text{out}}\left(\omega_\mu^t, y_\mu, V_\mu^t\right) X_{\mu i} v_i^t \end{aligned} \]

因此式 (\(\ref{eq:omega}\)) 化简为 \[ \omega_\mu^{t+1}=\sum_i X_{\mu i} \hat{w}_i^t-\sum_i f_{\text{out}}\left(\omega_\mu^t, y_\mu, V_\mu^t\right) X_{\mu i}^2 v_i^t=\sum_i X_{\mu i} \hat{w}_i-V_\mu^t f_{\mathrm{out}}\left(\omega_\mu^t, y_\mu, V_\mu^t\right) \]

GAMP推导完毕，我们将 \(\mathbf{X}\) 吸收的 scaling \(1/\sqrt{n}\) 重新标注出来，并将各式总结如下

初始化 \(\hat{w}^{0}_i,v^{0}_i,f_{\mathrm{out}}^{0}\) \[ \begin{aligned} & V^{t+1}_{\mu} = \frac{1}{n} \sum_i X_{\mu i}^2 v_i^t \\ & \omega_\mu^{t+1} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{n}} \sum_i X_{\mu i} \hat{w}^{t}_i-V_\mu^t f_{\mathrm{out}}^{t} \\ & f_{\text{out}}^{t+1} = f_{\text{out}} (y, \omega^{t+1}, V^{t+1}) \\ & \Sigma_i^{t+1} = \left[-\frac{1}{n}\sum_\mu X_{\mu i}^2 \partial_\omega f_{\mathrm{out}}\left(\omega_\mu^{t+1}, y_\mu, V_\mu^{t+1}\right)\right]^{-1} \\ & R_i^{t+1} = \hat{w}_i^t+\frac{1}{\sqrt{n}}\left(\Sigma_i\right)^{t+1} \sum_\mu X_{\mu i} f_{\mathrm{out}}\left(\omega_\mu^{t+1}, y_\mu, V_\mu^{t+1}\right) \\ &\hat{w}^{t+1}_i = \frac{\Sigma^{t+1}_i}{1+R^{t+1}_i} \\ &v^{t+1}_i = \frac{1}{1+R^{t+1}_i} \end{aligned} \]

其中，

\[ \begin{aligned} & \mathcal{Z}_{\text {out }^{\star}}(y, \omega, V) =\mathcal{N}_y\left(1, \Delta^{\star}\right) \frac{1}{2}\left(1+\operatorname{erf}\left(\frac{\omega}{\sqrt{2 V}}\right)\right)+\mathcal{N}_y\left(-1, \Delta^{\star}\right) \frac{1}{2}\left(1-\operatorname{erf}\left(\frac{\omega}{\sqrt{2 V}}\right)\right) \\ & \partial_\omega f_{\text{out}}(\omega, y, V) = \frac{\int \mathrm{d} z P_{\text {out }}(y | z)(z-\omega)^2 e^{-\frac{(z-\omega)^2}{2 V}}}{V^2 \int \mathrm{d} z P_{\text {out }}(y | z) e^{-\frac{(z-\omega)^2}{2 V}}} - \frac{1}{V} - f_{\text{out}}^2(\omega, y, V) \end{aligned} \]

4. 复本对称解

4.1 复本技巧

在贝叶斯最优框架下，写出配分函数 \[ \begin{equation} \mathcal{Z}(\mathbf{y}, \mathbf{X})=P(\mathbf{y},\mathbf{X}) = \int \mathrm{d}\mathbf{w}~ P(\mathbf{w}, \mathbf{y}, \mathbf{X})= \int \mathrm{d}\mathbf{w}~ P(\mathbf{y} | \mathbf{w}, \mathbf{X}) P(\mathbf{w}) \end{equation} \]

为了方便，引入变量 \(\mathbf{z}=\mathbf{w} \mathbf{X} / \sqrt{n}\)，因此有

\[ \begin{equation} P(\mathbf{y}|\mathbf{z}) = \int \mathrm{d}\mathbf{z}~ P(\mathbf{y} | \mathbf{w}, \mathbf{X})~ \delta\left( \mathbf{z} - \frac{1}{\sqrt{n}}\mathbf{w} \mathbf{X} \right) \end{equation} \] 配分函数改写为

\[ \begin{equation} \mathcal{Z}(\mathbf{y}, \mathbf{X}) = \int \mathrm{d}\mathbf{z}~ P(\mathbf{y} | \mathbf{z}) \int \mathrm{d}\mathbf{w}~ P(\mathbf{w}) ~ \delta\left( \mathbf{z} - \frac{1}{\sqrt{n}}\mathbf{w} \mathbf{X} \right) \end{equation} \]

为了求解自由能的淬火平均

\[ \begin{equation} \Phi = \frac{1}{n} \mathbb{E}_{\mathbf{y},\mathbf{X}}~ \log \mathcal{Z}(\mathbf{y}, \boldsymbol{X}) \end{equation} \]

使用复本方法将对 \(\log \mathcal{Z}(\mathbf{y}, \boldsymbol{X})\) 的平均转为对 \(\mathcal{Z}(\mathbf{y}, \boldsymbol{X})^r\) 的平均

\[ \begin{equation} \Phi = \frac{1}{n} \lim_{r\to 0} \frac{\partial \log \mathbb{E}_{\mathbf{y},\mathbf{X}}~ \left[ \mathcal{Z}(\mathbf{y}, \boldsymbol{X})^r \right]}{\partial r} \end{equation} \]

其中 \(r\) 是复本指标。下面求解复本平均 \(\mathbb{E}_{\mathbf{y},\mathbf{X}}~ \left[ \mathcal{Z}(\mathbf{y}, \boldsymbol{X})^r \right]\)

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} &\mathbb{E}_{\mathbf{y},\mathbf{X}}\left[\mathcal{Z}(\mathbf{y}, \mathbf{X})^r\right]\\ &= \mathbb{E}_{\mathbf{w}^\star,\mathbf{X}}\left[\prod_{a=1}^r \int_{\mathbb{R}^n} \mathrm{~d} \mathbf{z}^a P_{\mathrm{out}^a}\left(\mathbf{y} \mid \mathbf{z}^a\right) \int_{\mathbb{R}^d} \mathrm{~d} \mathbf{w}^a P_{\mathbf{w}^a}\left(\mathbf{w}^a\right) \delta\left(\mathbf{z}^a-\frac{1}{\sqrt{n}} \mathbf{X} \mathbf{w}^a\right)\right] \\ &= \mathbb{E}_{\mathbf{X}} \int_{\mathbb{R}^n} \mathrm{~d} \mathbf{y} \int_{\mathbb{R}^n} \mathrm{~d} \mathbf{z}^{\star} P_{\mathrm{out}^{\star}}\left(\mathbf{y} \mid \mathbf{z}^{\star}\right) \int_{\mathbb{R}^d} \mathrm{~d} \mathbf{w}^{\star} P_{\mathbf{w}^{\star}}\left(\mathbf{w}^{\star}\right) \delta\left(\mathbf{z}^{\star}-\frac{1}{\sqrt{n}} \mathbf{X} \mathbf{w}^{\star}\right) \\ & \times\left[\prod_{a=1}^r \int_{\mathbb{R}^n} \mathrm{~d} \mathbf{z}^a P_{\mathrm{out}^a}\left(\mathbf{y} \mid \mathbf{z}^a\right) \int_{\mathbb{R}^d} \mathrm{~d} \mathbf{w}^a P_{\mathbf{w}^a}\left(\mathbf{w}^a\right) \delta\left(\mathbf{z}^a-\frac{1}{\sqrt{n}} \mathbf{X} \mathbf{w}^a\right)\right] \\ &= \mathbb{E}_{\mathbf{X}} \int_{\mathbb{R}^n} \mathrm{~d} \mathbf{y} \prod_{a=0}^r \int_{\mathbb{R}^n} \mathrm{~d} \mathbf{z}^a P_{\mathrm{out}^a}\left(\mathbf{y} \mid \mathbf{z}^a\right) \int_{\mathbb{R}^d} \mathrm{~d} \mathbf{w}^a P_{\mathbf{w}^a}\left(\mathbf{w}^a\right) \delta\left(\mathbf{z}^a-\frac{1}{\sqrt{n}} \mathbf{X} \mathbf{w}^a\right) \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

其中，对 \(y\) 的平均是由老师的权重 \(w^\star\) 带来的，将其设为第零个复本。假设所有的数据 \(\mathrm{X}\) 都是独立同分布的，根据中心极限定理，有

\[ \begin{equation} z_\mu^a= \frac{1}{\sqrt{n}} \sum_{i=1}^n x_i^{(\mu)} w_i^a \sim \mathcal{N}\left( \mathbb{E}_{\mathrm{X}}\left[z_\mu^a\right], \mathbb{E}_{\mathrm{X}}\left[z_\mu^a z_\mu^b\right] \right) \end{equation} \]

其中

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} & \mathbb{E}_{\mathrm{X}}\left[z_\mu^a\right]=\frac{1}{\sqrt{n}} \sum_{i=1}^n \mathbb{E}_{\mathrm{X}}\left[x_i^{(\mu)}\right] w_i^a=0 \\ & \mathbb{E}_{\mathrm{X}}\left[z_\mu^a z_\mu^b\right]=\frac{1}{n} \sum_{i j} \mathbb{E}_{\mathrm{X}}\left[x_i^{(\mu)} x_j^{(\mu)}\right] w_i^a w_j^b=\frac{1}{n} \sum_{i j} \delta_{i j} w_i^a w_j^b=\frac{1}{n} \mathbf{w}^a \cdot \mathbf{w}^b \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

定义对称重叠矩阵（symmetric overlap matrix）

\[ \begin{equation} Q\left(\left\{\mathbf{w}^a\right\}\right) \equiv\left(\frac{1}{n} \mathbf{w}^a \cdot \mathbf{w}^b\right)_{a, b=0 . . r} \end{equation} \] 以及 \(\tilde{\mathbf{z}}_\mu \equiv\left(z_\mu^a\right)_{a=0\cdots r}\)，\(\tilde{\mathbf{w}}_i \equiv \left(w_i^a\right)_{a=0\cdots r}\)，因此有

\[ \begin{equation} \tilde{\mathbf{z}}_\mu \sim P_{\tilde{\mathbf{z}}}(\tilde{\mathbf{z}} ; Q)=\mathcal{N}_{\tilde{\mathbf{z}}}\left(\mathbf{0}_{r+1}, Q\right) \qquad P_{\tilde{\mathrm{w}}}(\tilde{\mathbf{w}})=\prod_{a=0}^r P_{\mathrm{w}}\left(\tilde{w}^a\right) \end{equation} \]

配分函数可以化为

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} \mathbb{E}_{\mathbf{y}, \mathbf{X}}\left[\mathcal{Z}(\mathbf{y}, \mathbf{X})^r\right] & =\mathbb{E}_{\mathbf{X}} \int \mathrm{~d} \mathbf{y} \prod_{a=0}^r \int \mathrm{~d} \mathbf{z}^a P_{\mathrm{out}^a}\left(\mathbf{y} \mid \mathbf{z}^a\right) \int \mathrm{~d} \mathbf{w}^a P_{\mathbf{w}^a}\left(\mathbf{w}^a\right) \delta\left(\mathbf{z}^a-\frac{1}{\sqrt{d}} \mathbf{X} \mathbf{w}^a\right) \\ & =\left[\int \mathrm{d} y \int \mathrm{~d} \tilde{\mathbf{z}} P_{\text{out}}(y \mid \tilde{\mathbf{z}}) P_{\tilde{\mathbf{z}}}(\tilde{\mathbf{z}} ; Q(\tilde{\mathbf{w}}))\right]^d\left[\int \mathrm{~d} \tilde{\mathbf{w}} P_{\tilde{\mathbf{w}}}(\tilde{\mathbf{w}})\right]^n \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

利用 \(\delta(x)\) 的傅里叶变换

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} 1 & =\int \mathrm{~d} Q \prod_{a \leq b} \delta\left(n Q_{a b}-\sum_{i=1}^n w_i^a w_i^b\right) \\ & \propto \int \mathrm{~d} Q \int \mathrm{~d} \hat{Q} \prod_{a \leq b} \exp\left\{ - \hat{Q}_{a b} \left(n Q_{a b}-\sum_{i=1}^n w_i^a w_i^b\right) \right\} \\ & \propto \int \mathrm{~d} Q \int \mathrm{~d} \hat{Q} \exp\left\{ -\sum_{a \leq b} \hat{Q}_{a b}\left(n Q_{a b}-\sum_{i=1}^n w_i^a w_i^b\right) \right\} \\ & \propto \int \mathrm{~d} Q \int \mathrm{~d} \hat{Q} \exp (-n \operatorname{Tr}[Q \hat{Q}]) \exp \left(\frac{1}{2} \sum_{i=1}^n \tilde{\mathbf{w}}_i^{\top} \hat{Q} \tilde{\mathbf{w}}_i\right) \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

可以将复本平均转为对 \(Q\) 和 \(\hat{Q}\) 的积分

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} \mathbb{E}_{\mathbf{y}, \mathbf{X}}\left[\mathcal{Z}(\mathbf{y}, \mathbf{X})^r\right] \propto& \int \mathrm{~d} Q \int \mathrm{~d} \hat{Q}~ \exp (-n \operatorname{Tr}[Q \hat{Q}]) \exp \left(\frac{1}{2} \sum_{i=1}^n \tilde{\mathbf{w}}_i^{\top} \hat{Q} \tilde{\mathbf{w}}_i\right)\\ & \left[\int \mathrm{d} y \int \mathrm{~d} \tilde{\mathbf{z}}~ P_{\text {out }}(y \mid \tilde{\mathbf{z}}) P_{\tilde{\mathbf{z}}}(\tilde{\mathbf{z}} ; Q(\tilde{\mathbf{w}}))\right]^d \left[\int \mathrm{~d} \tilde{\mathbf{w}} P_{\tilde{\mathbf{w}}}(\tilde{\mathbf{w}})\right]^n \\ \propto & \iint \mathrm{~d} Q \mathrm{~d} \hat{Q} ~ e^{n \Phi^{(r)}(Q, \hat{Q})} \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

其中，

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} &\Phi^{(r)}(Q, \hat{Q})=-\operatorname{Tr}[Q \hat{Q}]+\log \Psi_{\mathrm{w}}^{(r)}(\hat{Q})+\alpha \log \Psi_{\text {out }}^{(r)}(Q) \\ &\Psi_{\mathrm{w}}^{(r)}(\hat{Q})=\int \mathrm{~d} \tilde{\mathbf{w}}~ P_{\tilde{\mathbf{w}}}(\tilde{\mathbf{w}}) e^{\frac{1}{2} \tilde{\mathbf{w}} \hat{Q} \tilde{\mathbf{w}}} \\ &\Psi_{\text {out }}^{(r)}(Q)=\int \mathrm{d} y \int \mathrm{~d} \tilde{\mathbf{z}}~ P_{\tilde{z}}(\tilde{\mathbf{z}} ; Q) P_{\text {out }}(y \mid \tilde{\mathbf{z}}) \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

以及，

\[ \begin{equation} P_{\tilde{z}}(\tilde{\mathbf{z}} ; Q)=\frac{e^{-\frac{1}{2} \tilde{\mathbf{z}}^{\mathrm{T}} Q^{-1} \tilde{\mathbf{z}}}}{\operatorname{det}(2 \pi Q)^{1 / 2}} \end{equation} \]

使用Laplace近似即可求解自由能

\[ \begin{equation} \Phi(\alpha)=\operatorname{extr}_{Q, \hat{Q}}\left\{\lim _{r \rightarrow 0} \frac{\partial \Phi^{(r)}(Q, \hat{Q})}{\partial r}\right\} \end{equation} \]

4.2 复本对称假设

接下来在复本对称假设下求解自由能的表达式。假设复本具有如下的对称形式 \[ \begin{equation} Q_{\mathrm{rs}}=\left(\begin{array}{cccc} Q^0 & m & \ldots & m \\ m & Q & \ldots & \ldots \\ \ldots & \ldots & \ldots & q \\ m & \ldots & q & Q \end{array}\right) \quad \hat{Q}_{\mathrm{rs}}=\left(\begin{array}{cccc} \hat{Q}^0 & \hat{m} & \ldots & \hat{m} \\ \hat{m} & -\frac{1}{2} \hat{Q} & \ldots & \ldots \\ \ldots & \ldots & \ldots & \hat{q} \\ \hat{m} & \ldots & \hat{q} & -\frac{1}{2} \hat{Q} \end{array}\right) \end{equation} \]

其中 \(Q^0 = \rho_{\mathbf{w}^\star} = \frac{1}{n} || \mathbf{w}^\star ||^2_2\) 是老师权重的overlap，\(m = \frac{1}{n} \mathbf{w}^a \cdot \mathbf{w}^\star\) 是老师与复本之间的overlap，\(Q = \frac{1}{n} || \mathbf{w}^a ||^2_2\) 是复本权重的overlap，\(q = \frac{1}{n} \mathbf{w}^a \cdot \mathbf{w}^b\) 是复本之间的overlap。

分别计算 \(\Phi^{(r)}(Q, \hat{Q})\) 中的三项

  • Trace term

\[ \begin{equation} \left.\operatorname{Tr}(Q \hat{Q})\right|_{\mathrm{rs}}=Q^0 \hat{Q}^0+r m \hat{m}-\frac{1}{2} r Q \hat{Q}+\frac{r(r-1)}{2} q \hat{q} \end{equation} \]

因此有

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} &\operatorname{extr} \left\{\lim _{r \rightarrow 0} \frac{\partial}{\partial r}\left( -\operatorname{Tr}[Q \hat{Q}] \right)\right\} \\ &= \operatorname{extr} \left\{\lim _{r \rightarrow 0} \frac{\partial}{\partial r} \left( -Q^0 \hat{Q}^0-r m \hat{m}+\frac{1}{2} r Q \hat{Q}-\frac{r(r-1)}{2} q \hat{q} \right)\right\} \\ &= \operatorname{extr} \left\{\lim _{r \rightarrow 0} \left(- m \hat{m}+\frac{1}{2} Q \hat{Q}-\left( r-\frac{1}{2} \right) q \hat{q} \right)\right\} \\ &= \operatorname{extr} \left\{ - m \hat{m}+\frac{1}{2} Q \hat{Q} +\frac{1}{2}q \hat{q} \right\} \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

  • Prior integral

求解 \(\Psi_{\mathrm{w}}^{(r)}(\hat{Q})\) 的关键在于将矩阵乘法拆分 \[ \tilde{\mathbf{w}} \hat{Q}_{\mathrm{rs}} \tilde{\mathbf{w}} = w^\star \hat{Q}^0 w^\star + 2\sum_{a=1}^r w^\star \hat{m} w^a - \left( \hat{Q} + \hat{q} \right) \sum_{a=1}^r \left( w^a \right)^2 + \hat{q} \left( \sum_{a=1}^r w^a \right)^2 \]

可得 \[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} \left.\Psi_{\mathrm{w}}^{(r)}(\hat{Q})\right|_{\mathrm{rs}} & =\int \mathbf{d} \tilde{\mathbf{w}} P_{\tilde{\mathbf{w}}}(\tilde{\mathbf{w}}) e^{\frac{1}{2} \tilde{\mathbf{w}} \hat{Q}_{\mathrm{rs}} \tilde{\mathbf{w}}} \\ & =\mathbb{E}_{w^{\star}} e^{\frac{1}{2} \hat{Q}^0\left(w^{\star}\right)^2} \int \mathbf{d} \tilde{\mathbf{w}} P_{\tilde{\mathbf{w}}}(\tilde{\mathbf{w}}) e^{w^{\star} \hat{m} \sum_{a=1}^r w^a-\frac{1}{2}(\hat{Q}+\hat{q}) \sum_{a=1}^r\left(\tilde{w}^a\right)^2+\frac{1}{2} \hat{q}\left(\sum_{a=1}^r w^a\right)^2} \\ & =\mathbb{E}_{\xi, w^{\star}} e^{\frac{1}{2} \hat{Q}^0\left(w^{\star}\right)^2}\left[\mathbb{E}_w \exp \left(\left[\hat{m} w^{\star} w-\frac{1}{2}(\hat{Q}+\hat{q}) w^2+\hat{q}^{1 / 2} \xi w\right]\right)\right]^r \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

因此 \[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} \Psi_{\mathrm{w}}(\hat{Q})&=\operatorname{extr} \left\{\lim _{r \rightarrow 0} \frac{\partial}{\partial r}\left( \log \Psi_{\mathrm{w}}^{(r)}(\hat{Q}) \right)\right\} \\ & =\operatorname{extr} \left\{\lim _{r \rightarrow 0} \frac{\partial}{\partial r} \mathbb{E}_{\xi, w^{\star}} {\color{red} r} \log \left[\mathbb{E}_w \exp \left(\hat{m} w^{\star} w-\frac{1}{2}(\hat{Q}+\hat{q}) w^2+\hat{q}^{1 / 2} \xi w \right)\right] \right\} \\ & = \operatorname{extr} \left\{ \mathbb{E}_{\xi, w^{\star}} \log \left[\mathbb{E}_w \exp \left(\hat{m} w^{\star} w-\frac{1}{2}(\hat{Q}+\hat{q}) w^2+\hat{q}^{1 / 2} \xi w\right)\right] \right\} \\ &= \operatorname{extr} \left\{ \mathbb{E}_{\xi, w^{\star}} \exp\left( -\frac{1}{2} \hat{q}^{-1}\hat{m}^2 (w^{\star})^2 + \xi \hat{q}^{-\frac{1}{2}} \hat{m} w^\star \right) \log \left[\mathbb{E}_w \exp \left(-\frac{1}{2}(\hat{Q}+\hat{q}) w^2+\hat{q}^{1 / 2} \xi w\right)\right] \right\} \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

最后一步为了解耦老师与学生的权重，用了变量替换

\[ \xi \leftarrow \xi + \hat{q}^{-\frac{1}{2}} \hat{m} w^\star \]

  • Channel integral

求解 \(\Psi_{\text {out }}^{(r)}(Q)\) 的思路与求解 \(\Psi_{\mathrm{w}}^{(r)}(\hat{Q})\) 相同，难点在于求解 \(P_{\tilde{z}}(\tilde{\mathbf{z}} ; Q)\) 中的逆矩阵

\[ Q_{\mathrm{rs}}^{-1}=\left[\begin{array}{llll} Q_{00}^{-1} & Q_{01}^{-1} & Q_{01}^{-1} & Q_{01}^{-1} \\ Q_{01}^{-1} & Q_{11}^{-1} & Q_{12}^{-1} & Q_{12}^{-1} \\ Q_{01}^{-1} & Q_{12}^{-1} & Q_{11}^{-1} & Q_{12}^{-1} \\ Q_{01}^{-1} & Q_{12}^{-1} & Q_{12}^{-1} & Q_{11}^{-1} \end{array}\right] \] 其中， \[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} Q_{00}^{-1} =& \left(Q^0-r m(Q+(r-1) q)^{-1} m\right)^{-1} \\ Q_{01}^{-1} =& -\left(Q^0-r m(Q+(r-1) q)^{-1} m\right)^{-1} m(q+(r-1) q)^{-1} \\ Q_{11}^{-1} =& (Q-q)^{-1}-(Q+(r-1) q)^{-1} q(Q-q)^{-1} \\ &+(Q+(r-1) q)^{-1} m\left(Q^0-r m(Q+(r-1) q)^{-1} m\right)^{-1} m(Q+(r-1) q)^{-1} \\ Q_{12}^{-1} =& -(Q+(r-1) q)^{-1} q(Q-q)^{-1} \\ &+(Q+(r-1) q)^{-1} m\left(Q-r m(Q+(r-1) q)^{-1} m\right)^{-1} m(Q+(r-1) q)^{-1} \\ \operatorname{det} Q_{\mathrm{rs}} =& (Q-q)^{r-1}(Q+(r-1) q)\left(Q^0-r m(Q+(r-1) q)^{-1} m\right) \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

因此有

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} \left.\Psi_{\text {out }}^{(r)}(Q)\right|_{\mathrm{rs}} & =\int \mathrm{d} y \int \mathrm{d} \tilde{\mathbf{z}} e^{-\frac{1}{2} \tilde{\mathbf{z}}^{\top} Q_{\mathrm{rs}}^{-1} \tilde{\mathbf{z}}-\frac{1}{2} \log \left(\operatorname{det}\left(2 \pi Q_{\mathrm{rs}}\right)\right)} P_{\mathrm{out}}(y \mid \tilde{\mathbf{z}}) \\ & =\mathbb{E}_{y, \xi} \mathrm{e}^{-\frac{1}{2} \log \left(\operatorname{det}\left(2 \pi Q_{\mathrm{rs}}\right)\right)} \\ & \times \int \mathrm{d} z^{\star} P_{\text {out }^{\star}}\left(y \mid z^{\star}\right) e^{-\frac{1}{2} Q_{00}^{-1}\left(z^{\star}\right)^2}\left[\int d z P_{\text {out }}(y \mid z) e^{-Q_{01}^{-1} z^{\star} z-\frac{1}{2}\left(Q_{11}^{-1}-Q_{12}^{-1}\right) z^2-Q_{12}^{-1 / 2} \xi z}\right]^r \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

采用与求解 \(\Psi_{\mathrm{w}}^{(r)}(\hat{Q})\) 类似的处理方式，得到

\[ \Psi_{\text {out }}\left(Q, m, q ; \rho_{\mathrm{w}^{\star}}\right) \equiv \mathbb{E}_{y, \xi}\left[\mathcal{Z}_{\text {out }^{\star}}\left(y, m q^{-1 / 2} \xi, \rho_{\mathrm{w}^{\star}}-m q^{-1} m\right) \log \mathcal{Z}_{\text {out }}\left(y, q^{1 / 2} \xi, Q-q\right)\right] \]

 

将三项结果相加，最终得到 \[ \begin{equation} \Phi_{\mathrm{rs}}(\alpha) =\operatorname{extr}_{Q, \hat{Q}, q, \hat{q}, m, \hat{m}}\left\{-m \hat{m}+\frac{1}{2} Q \hat{Q}+\frac{1}{2} q \hat{q}+\Psi_{\mathbf{w}}(\hat{Q}, \hat{m}, \hat{q})+\alpha \Psi_{\text {out }}\left(Q, m, q ; \rho_{\mathrm{w}^{\star}}\right)\right\} \end{equation} \]

其中， \[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} &\Psi_{\mathrm{w}}(\hat{Q}, \hat{m}, \hat{q}) \equiv \mathbb{E}_{\xi}\left[\mathcal{Z}_{\mathrm{w}^{\star}}\left(\hat{m} \hat{q}^{-1 / 2} \xi, \hat{m} \hat{q}^{-1} \hat{m}\right) \log \mathcal{Z}_{\mathrm{w}}\left(\hat{q}^{1 / 2} \xi, \hat{Q}+\hat{q}\right)\right] \\ &\Psi_{\text {out }}\left(Q, m, q ; \rho_{\mathrm{w}^{\star}}\right) \equiv \mathbb{E}_{y, \xi}\left[\mathcal{Z}_{\mathrm{out}^{\star}}\left(y, m q^{-1 / 2} \xi, \rho_{\mathrm{w}^{\star}}-m q^{-1} m\right) \log \mathcal{Z}_{\mathrm{out}}\left(y, q^{1 / 2} \xi, Q-q\right)\right]\\ & \rho_{\mathbf{w}^{\star}}=Q^0=\lim _{n \rightarrow \infty} \mathbb{E}_{\mathbf{w}^{\star}} \frac{1}{n}\left\|\mathbf{w}^{\star}\right\|_2^2 \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

在贝叶斯最优中，有 \[ \begin{equation} \mathcal{Z}_{\text {out }}=\mathcal{Z}_{\text {out }^{\star}} \qquad \mathcal{Z}_{\mathbf{w}}=\mathcal{Z}_{\mathbf{w}^{\star}} \end{equation} \]

并且根据 Nishimori conditions 有 \[ \begin{equation} Q=\rho_{\mathrm{w}^{\star}},\quad m=q=q,\quad \hat{Q}=0, \quad \hat{m}=\hat{q}=\hat{q} \end{equation} \]

复本对称自由能简化为

\[ \begin{equation} \Phi(\alpha)=\operatorname{extr}_{q, \hat{q}}\left\{-\frac{1}{2} q \hat{q}+\Psi_{\mathrm{w}}^{\mathrm{b}}\left(\hat{q}\right)+\alpha \Psi_{\text {out }}^{\mathrm{b}}\left(q ; \rho_{\mathrm{w}^{\star}}\right)\right\} \end{equation} \]

其中， \[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} & \Psi_{\mathrm{w}}(\hat{q}) =\mathbb{E}_{\xi}\left[\mathcal{Z}_{\mathrm{w}^{\star}}\left(\hat{q}^{1 / 2} \xi, \hat{q}\right) \log \mathcal{Z}_{\mathrm{w}^{\star}}\left(\hat{q}^{1 / 2} \xi, \hat{q}\right)\right], \\ & \Psi_{\text {out }}\left(q ; \rho_{\mathrm{w}^{\star}}\right)=\mathbb{E}_{y, \xi}\left[\mathcal{Z}_{\text {out }^{\star}}\left(y, q^{1 / 2} \xi, \rho_{\mathrm{w}^{\star}}-q\right) \log \mathcal{Z}_{\text {out }^{\star}}\left(y, q^{1 / 2} \xi, \rho_{\mathrm{w}^{\star}}-q\right)\right] \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

通过

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{array}{rlrl} \hat{Q} & =-2 \alpha \partial_Q \Psi_{\text {out }}, && Q=-2 \partial_{\hat{Q}} \Psi_{\mathrm{w}} \\ \hat{q} & =-2 \alpha \partial_q \Psi_{\text {out }}, && q=-2 \partial_{\hat{q}} \Psi_{\mathrm{w}} \\ \hat{m} & =\alpha \partial_m \Psi_{\text {out }}, & &m=\partial_{\hat{m}} \Psi_{\mathrm{w}} \end{array} \end{equation} \]

可以分别求得各个序参量，但是在本问题中，根据 Nishimori conditions，只需要求出 \(q\) 和 \(\hat{q}\)。

在求偏导时定义辅助变量

\[ \begin{aligned} & f_{\mathrm{w}^{\star}}(\gamma, \Lambda) \equiv \partial_\gamma \log \mathcal{Z}_{\mathrm{w}^{\star}}(\gamma, \Lambda)=\frac{\gamma}{1+\Lambda} \\ & f_{\text {out }^{\star}}(y, \omega, V) \equiv \partial_\omega \log \mathcal{Z}_{\text {out }^{\star}}(y, \omega, V)=\frac{\mathcal{N}_y\left(1, \Delta^{\star}\right)-\mathcal{N}_y\left(-1, \Delta^{\star}\right)}{\mathcal{Z}_{\text {out }\star} (y, \omega, V)} \mathcal{N}_\omega(0, V) \end{aligned} \]

可得（计算过程待以后补充）

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} & \hat{q}=\alpha \mathbb{E}_{y, \xi}\left[\mathcal{Z}_{\mathrm{out}^{\star}}\left(y, q^{1 / 2} \xi, \rho_{\mathrm{w}^{\star}}-q\right) f_{\mathrm{out}^{\star}}\left(y, q^{1 / 2} \xi, \rho_{\mathrm{w}^{\star}}-q\right)^2\right] \\ & q=\mathbb{E}_{\xi}\left[\mathcal{Z}_{\mathrm{w}^{\star}}\left(\hat{q}^{1 / 2} \xi, \hat{q}\right) f_{\mathrm{w}^{\star}}\left(\hat{q}^{1 / 2} \xi, \hat{q}\right)^2\right] \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

为了给出 \(\mathcal{Z}_{\mathrm{out}}\) 的解析形式，我们用一个高斯分布取极限的形式来代替 \(\delta\) 函数

\[ \begin{equation} P(y \mid z)=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2 \pi \Delta}} \exp \left(\frac{y-\operatorname{sign}(z)}{2 \Delta}\right), \Delta \rightarrow \infty \end{equation} \]

因此

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} \mathcal{Z}_{\text {out }}(y, \omega, V)= & \int d z \frac{1}{\sqrt{2 \pi \Delta}} \exp \left(\frac{y-\operatorname{sign}(z)}{2 \Delta}\right) \frac{1}{\sqrt{2 \pi V}} \exp \left(-\frac{(z-\omega)^2}{2 V}\right) \\ = & \int_0^{\infty} d z \frac{1}{\sqrt{2 \pi \Delta}} \exp \left(\frac{y-1}{2 \Delta}\right) \frac{1}{\sqrt{2 \pi V}} \exp \left(-\frac{(z-\omega)^2}{2 V}\right) \\ + & \int_{-\infty}^0 d z \frac{1}{\sqrt{2 \pi \Delta}} \exp \left(\frac{y+1}{2 \Delta}\right) \frac{1}{\sqrt{2 \pi V}} \exp \left(-\frac{(z-\omega)^2}{2 V}\right) \\ = & \mathcal{N}_y\left(1, \Delta^*\right) \left(1+\operatorname{erf}\left(\frac{\omega}{\sqrt{2 V}}\right)\right)+\mathcal{N}_y\left(-1, \Delta^*\right) \frac{1}{2}\left(1-\operatorname{erf}\left(\frac{\omega}{\sqrt{2 V}}\right)\right) \\ f_{\text {out }^{\star}}(y, \omega, V) =& \frac{\mathcal{N}_y\left(1, \Delta^{\star}\right)-\mathcal{N}_y\left(-1, \Delta^{\star}\right)}{\mathcal{Z}_{\text {out }^{\star}}(y, \omega, V)} \mathcal{N}_\omega(0, V) \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

同样的，在 \(P_{\mathbf{w}^\star} \sim \mathcal{N}(0,1)\) 时，有 \[ \begin{equation} \mathcal{Z}_{\mathrm{w}^{\star}}(\gamma, \Lambda)=\frac{e^{\frac{\gamma^2}{2(\Lambda +1)}}}{\sqrt{\Lambda +1}}, \quad f_{\mathrm{w}^{\star}}(\gamma, \Lambda)=\frac{\gamma}{1+\Lambda}, \quad \partial_\gamma f_{\mathrm{w}^{\star}}(\gamma, \Lambda)=\frac{1}{1+\Lambda} \end{equation} \]

因此

\[ \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} q &=\frac{\hat{q}}{1+\hat{q}} \\ \hat{q} & =\alpha \mathbb{E}_{y, \xi}\left[\mathcal{Z}_{\text {out }^{\star}}\left(y, q^{1 / 2} \xi, 1-q\right) f_{\text {out }^{\star}}\left(y, q^{1 / 2} \xi, 1-q\right)^2\right] \\ & =2 \alpha \int D \xi y^2 \frac{\mathcal{N}_{\sqrt{q} \xi}\left(0,1-q\right)^2}{\frac{1}{2}\left(1+\operatorname{erf}\left(\frac{\sqrt{q \xi}}{\sqrt{2\left(1-q\right)}}\right)\right)}\\ &=\frac{2}{\pi} \frac{\alpha}{1-q} \int D \xi \frac{e^{-\frac{q_{\mathrm{b} \xi^2}}{1-q}}}{\left(1+\operatorname{erf}\left(\frac{\sqrt{q \xi}}{\sqrt{2\left(1-q\right)}}\right)\right)} \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]
